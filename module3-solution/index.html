Chicken

Here is a collection of recipes using chicken. One of the first domesticated animals, chicken is the most popular type of fowl in the world[1]. A significant portion of the meat and eggs used in human nutrition come from chickens. It can be prepared in a wide range of ways for cuisine, depending on the region and culture. Due to their widespread use and ease of growing, chickens are practically entirely edible.Beginning around 600 BC, carvings in Babylonia show chicken as a meat. Among the available meats in the Middle Ages, chicken was one of the most popular. It was consumed over the majority of the Eastern hemisphere, along with a variety of chicken species, including capons, pullets, and hens.The so-called "white dish," a stew often made with chicken and fried onions simmered in milk and seasoned with spices and sugar, used it as one of its primary ingredients.
